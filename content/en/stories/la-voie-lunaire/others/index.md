---
title: "Glossary"
---

Argrath: name spoken by the Tarshite Exile (a savior, a rebel leader, a prophet?)

Brontos: Storm Bull of the Apple Clan

Cai Delli: ogre

Caius Brontex: magistrate at Bagnot (friend of Fazzur)

Cherusii: Sartarite clan (whose leader is Farindar)

Clousilex: name given by Isidilian as a dwarven contact at Pavis

Elemenoria: witch of the Falling Ruins

Farindar: leader of the Cherus clan

Fazzur the Learned: conqueror of Prax

Gartinor: a grey sage cited by Irken as practicing divination

Gazzams: sort of dinosaurs

Gomax: lunar officer posted at Glasswall

Goundar: dwarf of Dwarf Mine

Grindaroum: dwarf (very very old) of Dwarf Mine having a Vision Stone.

Harvar Iron Fist: duke of AldaChur allied to the Lunaries and converted to Yelmalio.

Hazz: lunar colonist who practices slave trading.

Hynir: Sartarite war thane

Imex Rapiis: tactical advisor to Pharandros (member of the Secret Phrases)

Irken: youth of the Apple Clan who lost his brother Riken killed by ogres.

Irmir: man of the Apple Clan

Isidilian: King of Dwarf Mine

Jaknar the Red: stole Dwarven weapons

Jakran: one of the warriors of the village at the foot of the cliff

Jarlan: leader of the Apple Clan

Jirudal: man of the Apple Clan

Jomes Wulf: man who fought the Telmoris (werewolves)

Kalia: woman of the Apple Clan

Korlan: the man of the Apple Clan who took the dwarven medallion

Lamars: enemy clan of the Cherusii (betrayed them during the lunar invasion)

Nigmar: one of the dwarven warriors of Dwarf Mine

Perandal: leader of the Princeros tribe based at Glasswall

Pizidan: man of the Apple Clan sitting on the Circle

Pommiers (Apple Clan): the apple clan. A Sartarite clan renowned for its consecrated apples.

Pharandros: young king of Tarsh and nephew of Fazzur

Sheena: healer priestess of the Apple Clan

Sigmar: leader of the village at the foot of the cliff

Urulvir: old Sartarite sage

Visa Delli: wife of Cai Delli (ogress)
