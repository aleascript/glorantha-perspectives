---
title: "Runic colors"
---

The world of Glorantha is vibrant, and this extends to physical attributes: skin, eyes, hair.

Indeed, one can link skin color, eye color, and hair color to the dominant rune.

These colors come from the Other Side. Thus, in HeroQuests, attributes can be amplified. One can even end up transformed at the conclusion of the quest.

An individual can be marked at birth or during their initiation, but also at the outcome of a HeroQuest.

Physical runic marks can also vary depending on certain events: after a ritual, during a festival, during this or that season...

Not all organs are necessarily marked. Some will have orange eyes, others bright red hair, others pale gray skin.

Here are some colors:

- **Air**: orange or blue
- **Earth**: olive green or brown
- **Moon**: red or white
- **Water**: blue or white
- **Darkness**: dull gray or black
- **Fire**: yellow or red

Other runes can also color a character.

> It is also possible to use these colors only during HeroQuests.
