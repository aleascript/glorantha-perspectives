---
title: "Runic forms"
---

Runes hold an essential place in Glorantha.

They are everywhere and constitute the world. The Scholars of the Ambigu formalized them, but they were already present from the very beginning.

In particular:

- Written or printed books are triangular
- Coins have the shape of the rune of Exchange among the Orlanthi
- Spiral forms (trees, stones, hills, mountains)
- Square fields (i.e. no rectangular fields!)
- Swords have the shape of the rune of Death
- Skins are marked with tattoos that appear during initiations or ceremonies. Gloranthans generally do not hide their runic affiliations (except for deceptive cults).

> On some points, we stray from Gloranthan canon. But certain forms can appear during a HeroQuest or deliberately embrace the [shift](../yet-another-glorantha).
