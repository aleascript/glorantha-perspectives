---
title: "Yet Another Glorantha (YAG)"
---

In response to **YGWV** (Your Glorantha Will Vary), here is an idea that could allow you to stray from Gloranthan canon in a completely deliberate way.

In a distant future (Harshax or even later), a HeroQuest was undertaken and it resulted in the mating of a Dragon with Arachne Solara. From this union, Arachne Solara laid thousands of eggs.

Each egg that hatches corresponds to a version of Glorantha.

The more veined the egg, the more different the version, with minor or even major differences from the official Glorantha.

Thus the HeroWars are totally open within this framework.

Furthermore, the Glorantha that hatches can also be very different (see for example the approach of putting runes everywhere in the shapes of everyday objects: books, fields, coins... or the approach regarding skin colors based on the birth rune).

The players are unaware that they are a version of the world born from an egg.
