---
title: "Glorantha for newbies"
---

For the **Gloranthix** ;) and with the goal of presenting certain aspects of the world to align a group of players.

- Characters not belonging to a **community** are extremely rare.
- The visual setting is equivalent to the Bronze Age. We are in an **ancient world** where one finds primitive, barbarian cultures and the beginnings of civilizations (lunar, solar, western...)
- **Runes** are everywhere: tattoos, engravings, frescoes, armor, weapons...
- **Magic** is **visible** (the air hums, an aura appears, sometimes the elements participate as well).
- Generally one **respects one's opponent (and life)** with the exception of Chaos.
- **Informal speech** is widespread (except in the West where there is a caste hierarchy).
- The world is rather **vibrant** except among the Brithinis (immortal fundamentalist Malkioni) and in certain wild regions.
- **Currency** is used for **exchanges**. It is rare and reserved for merchants and certain adventurous professions. Otherwise, barter is the norm in everyday life along with the use of small change.
- **Healing** delays death, speeds recovery, but does not regrow limbs.
