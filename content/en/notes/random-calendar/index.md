title: "Random calendar"
---

It can be practical to roll a day in the Gloranthan calendar.

The most common Gloranthan calendar divides the year into days, weeks, and seasons with a two-week period called Sacred Time.

Here we present the different names used in the calendar as well as a simple way to determine a day, week, or season by dice.

For reference, the day is important for lunar magic outside the Brillant Line, a line that encircles the Lunar Empire and that the Empire seeks to extend because within this line, the moon is always full and thus magic is not subject to the cycle as it is outside.


# The days
Roll 2d6 (use the result of the 1st die unless you roll doubles)
        
* [1] **Frost**: last crescent (darkness)
* [2] **Water**: waning moon (water)
* [3] **Clay**: new moon (earth)
* [4] **Wind**: first crescent (air)
* [5] **Fire**: first quarter (fire)
* [6] **Wild**: full moon (moon)
* [Double] **Divine**: last quarter

# The weeks
Roll 1D8. Note that this can also be used to determine a rune of power.

* [1] **Disorder**
* [2] **Harmony**
* [3] **Death**
* [4] **Fertility**
* [5] **Stasis**
* [6] **Movement**
* [7] **Illusion**
* [8] **Truth**

> The Theyalan Grey Seers like to reflect on the order of the weeks in relation to the associated [runes of power](../runes-meditation).

# The seasons

Roll 2D20 (use the result of the 1st die unless you roll doubles). This can also be used to determine an Element Rune (on doubles, it's the rune of the Moon).

* [1-4] **Sea**: corresponds to spring
* [5-8] **Fire**: corresponds to summer
* [9-12] **Earth**: corresponds to the end of summer and beginning of autumn
* [13-16] **Dark**: corresponds to winter
* [17-20] **Storms**: corresponds to a very windy and turbulent end of winter
* [Double] **Sacred Time**: if even double, week of Chance; if odd double, week of Destiny. Corresponds to a period of important rituals to perform for the new year.
