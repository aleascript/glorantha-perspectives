> **A note on AI**: I have no talent for drawing and I thank the talented and wonderful artists who have managed to depict the world of Glorantha. May Sestarto grant them his blessing! However, there isn't always an illustration for everything that could be imagined. So, at the risk of incurring the wrath of anti-AI voices, I allow myself to use AI to generate visuals for this site. That said, I fully understand that any illustration may go against the imagination of a Glorantha explorer, and one does not extend the same leniency toward AI as toward the effort of another human being, even if that person's vision doesn't necessarily match what one would have imagined for Glorantha. I am therefore still hesitant about whether or not to provide illustrations for the stories.
