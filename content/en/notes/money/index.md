Some notes on certain currencies in circulation in Glorantha.

Glorantha is a world made of communities. Currency is less widespread than in our current world. Currencies are therefore rather rare and mostly the province of Princes or urban populations.

Trade is the domain of certain cults that regulate exchanges. The majority of people practice barter or use small change.

Adventurers may come across divine coins but will likely need to find a priest to exchange them for small change for their everyday transactions. As priests are the guarantors of the order of exchanges, they may want to know the origin of the divine currency.

## Divine Currencies

These currencies are used by religious authorities (initiates, priests...) to regulate major important exchanges within the Community. As usual, lunar currencies are the exception, and due to the inclusive nature of the Red Moon, Moons and Stars are used by everyone within the Empire with the goal of eventually replacing all other currencies.

- **Guilders**: in bronze, in the shape of the rune of Exchange to create slabs easier to transport and store. Reserved for initiates of Issaries who manage exchanges.

- **Moons**: in silver, in the shape of the rune of the Moon. Used in the Empire but more open than Guilders. Thus Moons are not reserved only for initiates. Lunars buy and sell exclusively in Moons or Stars. This is part of their strategy of inclusion.

- **Wheels**: in gold, large round coins shaped like the rune of Yelm. Currency used by the Dara Happans. Rare. Reserved for the elite. Worth ten times as much as a guilder or a moon.

- **Bolgs**: pierced pieces of lead, used by the Uz. Worth a hundred times less than a Guilder or a Moon.

## Small Change

Since the majority of people do not have access to divine currencies, there is small change to complement barter.

- **Coins**: in copper, small round coins for everyday use. Worth a hundred times less than a Guilder or a Moon.
- **Stars**: in aluminum, star-shaped. Used in the Lunar Empire for small values. Worth a hundred times less than a Moon.
- **Clacks**: pierced stones, for everyday use among the Uz. Worth a hundred times less than a Bolg.
