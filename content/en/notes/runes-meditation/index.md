title: Meditation on the runes
---

> Scholars (Grey Seers of Lankhor Mhy, Brown Seers of Irippi Ontor, Logicians of the Invisible God, Buserian Astrologers...) love to spend hours reflecting on the order of the runes of power.

 
 The Buserian Astrologers have a well-established idea of the order of these runes in the Cosmos. They meditate on their order starting from one of the eight runes and traversing the circle in one direction or the other (*"The 16 Traversals in the Sky"*)

 The Theyalans enjoy discussing the order of the weeks of the eponymous calendar: Disorder, Harmony, Death, Fertility, Stasis, Movement, Illusion, Truth.

 Others are more open, and like to randomly draw the order and find the link leading to the next. They refine their wisdom to dispense the best counsel to rulers.

 Some also use it as an examination in the Universities of the great Cities.

Two examples of meditations:

> **Life** leads to **harmony** and creates an era of **true** peace. There is nothing to add: things are **fixed**. But this leads to non-life (**death**). This break brings **disorder**. From this disorder, one discovers **illusion**. And this illusion is the source of **movement** which can lead again to a **fertile** period.

> **Life** is **movement**. But after a while, one can no longer find one's way. This leads to **disorder**. From this disorder, **death** and destruction appear. This causes astonishment and marks a time of **stasis**. This allows one to reassess the **truth** of things and lead back to **harmony** which will nourish **life** and restart the cycle.
