---
title: "Multiple frameworks of thought for the same character?"
---

![Maitrise](../../../assets/rules/runes/conditions/maitrise.png)


Glorantha is a complex world and syncretisms can emerge. Ideas and points of view circulate. It is therefore entirely possible for a character to use multiple frameworks of thought, especially the Lunars who are of an inclusive nature. This was also likely the case for the Scholars of the Ambigu in the 2nd Age or Arkat in the 1st Age.

However, it is worth noting that these syncretisms are rare.
