## Glorantha

Your first source of inspiration is Glorantha. Research it. Read. Appreciate. Digest. Discuss. This is what allows you to say: this thing is possible in Glorantha or not. Knowing that there are exceptions, often, everywhere. It is a rich and complex world.

The keywords for building strategies, objectives, questions, and of course for constructing stakes to use in contests come naturally from reading the lore. The HW and HQ supplements are in fact models of the genre with almost no stats: pure lore directly usable, and many RuneQuest supplements essentially contain lore.

That said, you can also draw from [runic inspiration](../../rules/runes/inspiration).
