One could even consider replacing the stakes with pebbles, bones, or pieces of wood drawn from a bag containing:

- 1/6 of objects marked with 3 spirit runes in red (the 6s)
- 2/6 of objects marked with two spirit runes for the 4s and one spirit rune for the 2s (in red)
- 2/6 of objects unmarked or with notches or colors (black for the 5s and green for the 3s)
- 1/6 of objects marked with the spirit rune or the Death rune in white (the 1s)

You would need many similar objects to prevent "cheating" and also to avoid having to reshuffle each time you search for a connection. Number to plan for: 60 provides equivalent coverage to 10 stakes (there are indeed 10 of each element).
