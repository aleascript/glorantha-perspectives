---
title: "Runic Cards"
---

![cards](../../../../assets/rules/cards.png)

One could replace the stakes with a deck of cards containing:
- 1/6 of the God's major rune (for 6s)
- 2/6 with minor runes of the pantheon or the God (for 2s and 4s)
- 2/6 with nothing or neutral runes (for 3s and 5s)
- 1/6 of the rune most opposed to the God (for 1s)

You would need many cards to prevent "cheating" and also to avoid having to reshuffle each time you seek runic support. Number to plan for: 60 provides equivalent coverage for camps of 10 stakes (there are indeed 10 of each element). The divine explosion is slightly contained but it is statistically negligible.
